Instant Ramen

Description

A long standing staple of college cuisine, instant ramen has filled stomachs and saved budgets around the world.

Bricks of noodles, made of wheat flour, palm oil, and salt, are dropped in boiling water and often seasoned with a flavor packet that come in countless varieties, to create a popular comfort food.

Ingredients

  • ramen brick
  • water

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with 2 cups of water.
  2. Set pot on the stove top and set to high.
  3. Once the water reaches a rolling boil, add the brick.
  4. After roughly 3 minutes, turn off the heat and add the flavor packet.
  5. Stir until the flavoring has completely dissolved.
  6. Pour into a bowl and enjoy once it has reached a comfortable temperature.
